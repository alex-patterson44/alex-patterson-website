Professional biography

My story in a nutshell

I do my best work when I can bring structure to ideas, help a team move forward, and communicate in a way that makes an audience want to keep listening. Over time, I have learned that credibility is built through consistency: showing up prepared, asking good questions, and following through.

Growth story

I started as a student focused mainly on completing assignments. Through upper-division coursework, group projects, and presentations, I have become more intentional about audience, clarity, and follow-through. I now approach communication as a skill that shapes trust.

Audience focus

This portfolio speaks directly to instructors, classmates, and future professional contacts. It invites you to explore my work, see how I solve problems, and contact me if you want a communicator who is prepared, adaptable, and easy to work with.

Call to action

Use the navigation to move between my resume, reflection page, and work samples. Each page adds a different part of the story so you can see both the results I have produced and the habits that shaped them.
