My Story

Built on clarity, consistency, and growth.

I started as a student focused on completing assignments well. Through upper-division coursework, group projects, and presentations, I became more intentional about audience, clarity, and follow-through. I now approach communication as a skill that shapes trust.

I've learned that credibility comes from consistency: showing up prepared, asking good questions, and following through on commitments. When I'm part of a team, people notice that I listen carefully, keep projects organized, and help others do their best work.

This site reflects who I am: someone who values clear structure, polished presentation, and making the next step obvious for whoever is reading or listening.
