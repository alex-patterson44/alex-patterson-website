My Story

Built on experience, values, and a commitment to excellence.

My Beginning
I came to the University of Arizona on a military scholarship through ROTC, where I initially pursued Mechanical and then Civil Engineering. The discipline, structure, and leadership that ROTC instilled in me became the foundation of everything I am professionally. Those years shaped my work ethic, my composure under pressure, and my ability to lead with purpose and intention.

My Transition
After immersing myself in the military path, I made the courageous decision to step back and realign with what truly drives me. I found my fiancée, a new sense of direction, and a calling that felt undeniably right — the world of accounting. That transition wasn't a setback; it was the most intentional and self-aware decision I have ever made. It was a leap of faith that led me to discover my passion for tax and my commitment to building a career where I can make a meaningful impact through strategic thinking, leadership, and clear communication.

My Future
I now study accounting at the Eller College of Management, one of the most respected business programs in the country. Through my studies, I discovered a genuine passion for tax — the strategy, the complexity, and the direct impact it has on people and organizations. I am actively positioning myself for a career in tax where I can combine my analytical mindset, my leadership ability, and my commitment to delivering results that matter. I am excited to continue growing, learning, and making an impact in the world of accounting and beyond.
