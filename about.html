My Story

I started my journey focused on doing class work well, then learned how much communication shapes outcomes in teams and projects. Through presentations, group work, and reflection, I built confidence in leading with clarity and consistency.

Today, I focus on being the teammate who keeps projects organized, communicates clearly, and helps others do their best work.

Core Values

Clarity

I keep communication direct and useful so people can act quickly and confidently.

Reliability

I follow through on commitments because trust is built through consistent action.

Growth

I use feedback to improve fast and turn every project into a learning opportunity.

Respect

I lead with professionalism and empathy so collaboration stays productive and positive.

Professional Background

I am a Business Communications student connected to the Eller College of Management, with experience in team-based coursework, professional presentations, and business writing.

My work reflects strong collaboration, audience awareness, and practical communication that supports real project outcomes.

Fun Facts
