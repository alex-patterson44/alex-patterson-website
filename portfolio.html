Team strategy presentation

Situation: The team needed to present a complex business topic to a class audience with limited time.

Task: I was responsible for organizing the message, building the slide flow, and making the delivery easy to follow.

Action: I structured the presentation around one main claim, wrote transitions, and rehearsed with the group until timing and tone were consistent.

Result: The presentation felt polished, the audience could follow the argument, and the team delivered with more confidence.
